# Content Security Risks (Overview)

## What is content security?
Content security focuses on protecting creative assets from unauthorized access, leaks, or misuse throughout the pitch-to-play lifecycle.

## Common risks
- Unauthorized access to scripts or rough cuts
- Leaks during post-production or vendor handoffs
- Over-permissioned accounts
- Human error and social engineering
